Our Story

Brewing more than coffee — brewing community, love, and culture

From a Single Bean to a Shared Dream

Jawi Coffee was born in the heart of Nairobi with a simple mission: to bring people together over a cup of locally sourced, perfectly brewed coffee. What started as a tiny corner shop with two passionate baristas has now grown into a cozy retreat known for its warmth, aroma, and authentic African blends.

"Every bean tells a story. At Jawi, we work directly with small-scale farmers across East Africa to ensure that every roast supports sustainability and fair trade."

Our team is made up of coffee lovers, artists, and dreamers — all working to create an experience you can feel in every sip. We believe coffee isn't just a drink — it's a culture. A conversation starter. A daily ritual. And above all, a reason to slow down and savor life's little moments.

From the highlands of Kenya to the bustling streets of Nairobi, our journey has been fueled by passion and purpose. We meticulously source our beans, roast them with care, and craft each cup to perfection. But more than that, we've created spaces where friendships are forged, ideas are born, and communities thrive.

Our Journey

2010

The Humble Beginnings

Founded by Sarah and James Kimani in a small 20m² shop in downtown Nairobi. Our first coffee machine was second-hand, but our passion was brand new.

2013

First Coffee Sourcing Trip

We visited small coffee farms in the Kenyan highlands, establishing direct relationships with growers committed to sustainable practices.

2015

Opening Our Roastery

We invested in our own roasting facility, allowing us to control every aspect of flavor development from bean to cup.

2018

Expansion to Second Location

Our popularity grew, leading to the opening of our second cafe in the vibrant Westlands neighborhood.

2023

Sustainability Milestone

We achieved 100% sustainable sourcing and became Kenya's first carbon-neutral coffee company.

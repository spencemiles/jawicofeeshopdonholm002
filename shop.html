Our Story

Founded in 2015, Jawi Coffee Shop began as a small passion project by two coffee enthusiasts who wanted to share their love for quality brews and homemade pastries.

Today, we've grown into a beloved local spot known for our artisanal approach to coffee and commitment to using only the finest ingredients in all our offerings.

Every cup of coffee is carefully crafted by our trained baristas, and our bakery items are made fresh daily using traditional recipes with a modern twist.
